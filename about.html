IR Formalization & Technical Approach

Resume = Query | Job Postings = Documents | Output = Ranked List

Text Processing

Extract skills, experience, and keywords; clean and normalize job descriptions.

➔

Vector Representation

TF-IDF and Word2Vec / BERT embeddings convert text into comparable numerical form.

➔

Cosine Similarity

Measures semantic closeness between resume vector and each job posting.

➔

Top-N Ranking

Jobs are ranked by relevance score; the highest matches are returned to the user.
